## Executive Overview

### Project Vision
Create a marketing portfolio web app that combines cutting-edge design with AI-driven marketing strategies, optimized for 2026 best practices. The app will serve as a showcase for professional services while implementing a no-backend architecture using static site generation and form services.

### BMAD Methodology Application
This plan applies the Breakthrough Method for Agile AI-Driven Development (BMAD) through:
1. Dual-stream market research (worldwide + Czech-specific)
2. Systematic synthesis of findings
3. Data-driven prioritization of features

### Success Criteria
- 90%+ alignment with 2026 marketing trends
- Seamless bilingual (Czech/English) functionality
- Top 3 ranking in Czech tech portfolio benchmarks
- 20% faster development through template reuse
