## Page-by-Page SEO Recommendations

### Homepage (/)

- Title: "DevOps & AI Consulting | [Name]"
- H1: Moderní DevOps a AI řešení
- Meta description: Focus on transformation, cost savings

### Services (/services)

- List each service with dedicated H2
- Include pricing where appropriate

### Projects (/projects)

- Use project names as H2
- Include metrics (40% cost reduction, etc.)

### Contact (/contact)

- Local SEO: Add Prague/Czech Republic
- Schema: LocalBusiness
