### 2.3 Business Culture

**Trust Indicators Valued**:

1. Professional certifications (AWS, Microsoft, Google Cloud)
2. Education background (Czech universities)
3. Local references and testimonials
4. GDPR compliance
5. Transparent pricing

**Pricing Expectations**:

- Hourly: $35-100/hr (avg $50/hr)
- Models: Project-based preferred, hybrid with hourly

**Source**: `02-czech-research/business-culture.md`

### 2.4 Marketing Practices

**SEO Requirements**:

- **Google.cz**: ~85% market share - primary target
- **Seznam.cz**: ~12% market share - local alternative
- Dual strategy required for maximum visibility

**Compliance**:

- GDPR: Mandatory privacy policy, explicit cookie consent
- Cookie banner: Equal accept/reject options required
- Data retention: Clear policies needed

**Source**: `02-czech-research/marketing-practices.md`

## 4. Strategic Synthesis

### 4.1 Key Differentiators

Based on all research, the portfolio should emphasize:

1. **"Partner for Digital Growth"** - Position as strategic partner, not just developer
2. **Performance as Proof** - Astro + VPS deployment showcases DevOps expertise
3. **Results-Oriented** - Include metrics, ROI, case studies
4. **Bilingual (CZ + EN)** - Czech primary for local trust, English for reach

## 5. Actionable Recommendations

### 5.1 Positioning

- Frame expertise (DevOps, AI, Web Dev) as solutions driving business growth
- Use "partner for digital growth" messaging
- Include specific metrics and outcomes in case studies

### 5.2 Technical

- Continue with Astro + Tailwind + VPS deployment
- Highlight deployment infrastructure as DevOps showcase
- Implement file-based i18n (CZ + EN)

### 5.3 SEO

- Create **Firmy.cz** profile immediately
- Optimize for both Google.cz and Seznam.cz
- Use Czech keywords in CZ version

### 5.4 Trust & Compliance

- Display IČO clearly
- Implement GDPR cookie banner (explicit opt-in)
- Add client logos and testimonials
- Ensure SSL certificate

### 5.5 Pricing

- Hybrid model: Fixed-price packages + hourly
- Start at $50/hr, adjust based on expertise
