## KPI Tracking Plan

### Tools
- **Google Analytics 4**: Primary analytics
- **Google Search Console**: SEO performance
- **Lighthouse CI**: Performance monitoring
- **Plausible**: Privacy-focused analytics (alternative)
- **Netlify Forms**: Form submission tracking
- **Manual Tracking**: Business metrics

### Reporting
- **Daily**: Automated email summaries
- **Weekly**: KPI dashboard review
- **Monthly**: Comprehensive KPI report
- **Quarterly**: KPI review and adjustment

### Alerts
- **Performance**: Alert if Lighthouse score <90
- **Conversion**: Alert if conversion rate <1% for 7 days
- **Traffic**: Alert if organic traffic drops >20%
