## MoSCoW Methodology

### Definitions
- **Must Have**: Critical for launch, non-negotiable
- **Should Have**: Important but not critical, can be deferred to v1.1
- **Could Have**: Nice to have if time permits, low priority
- **Won't Have**: Out of scope for current project, future consideration

### Prioritization Criteria
- **Business Value**: Impact on goals and objectives
- **User Value**: Impact on user experience and satisfaction
- **Technical Feasibility**: Ease of implementation
- **Cost**: Resource requirements
- **Risk**: Potential issues or dependencies
