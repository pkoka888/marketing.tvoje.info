# Czech Market Needs Analysis 2026: Marketing Automation & AI

**Source**: Analysis of job offers (Jobs.cz, StartupJobs.cz, LinkedIn) and industry trends.

## 1. Core Demand: "Strategic Orchestration"

Companies are no longer looking for just "execution" (running ads) but for **system orchestration**.

- **Job Title Trends**: "Marketing Automation Specialist", "AI Marketing Manager", "Growth Engineer", "Revenue Intelligence Architect".
- **Key Pain Point**: Fragmented data. Companies have HubSpot, Google Ads, E-shop data, and AI tools, but they don't talk to each other.
- **Goal**: Unified customer journeys and "Hyper-personalization".

## 3. The "Missing Link" Roles & Skills

Job descriptions highlight gaps that our agency/platform can fill:

- **"Data Translator"**: Explaining AI insights to C-level.
- **"Prompt Engineer / Vibe Coder"**: Translating business goals into AI instructions.
- **"Process Architect"**: Mapping customer journeys before automation.
- **"Ethical AI Governance"**: Knowing GDPR/AI Act compliance.
