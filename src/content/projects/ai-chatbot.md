## The Challenge

A rapidly growing e-commerce company faced overwhelming support tickets. Customers waited hours for responses during peak periods, leading to poor satisfaction scores and lost sales.

## Our Strategy

We deployed an intelligent AI chatbot solution:

1. **ChatGPT Integration**: Fine-tuned model on company knowledge base and past conversations.
2. **Multi-Channel Deployment**: Connected to website, Facebook Messenger, and WhatsApp.
3. **Smart Routing**: Escalated complex issues to human agents seamlessly.
4. **Continuous Learning**: Implemented feedback loops for ongoing improvement.

## The Results

- **70% Reduction** in support ticket volume.
- **24/7 Availability** for customer inquiries worldwide.
- **90% Customer Satisfaction** score for chatbot interactions.
- **40% Cost Savings** in customer support operations.
